About us

Our focus is

Promolecular Sciences is dedicated to pioneering research and development to create innovative enzymes and diagnostic platforms. We specialize in delivering efficient contract research services for the Biotech and Research sectors. Our focus extends to deploying life-science solutions using advanced technologies like Gene and mRNA synthesis through platforms such as BIOXP from Telesis Bio. Our comprehensive R&D services span concept design, proof of concept, and scalable projects across diverse fields. We're committed to leveraging Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) technology for genome analysis of Indian strains, particularly in agriculture and dye industries. This empowers us to discover and develop new enzymes and proteins for agriculture, veterinary, and human diagnostics. We also actively pursue technology and protocol development to enhance productivity in the Agricultural and Human Diagnostic industries.
