About us

Our focus is

Promolecular Sciences is dedicated to pioneering research and development to create innovative enzymes and diagnostic platforms. We specialize in delivering efficient contract research services for the Biotech and Research sectors. Our focus extends to deploying life-science solutions using advanced technologies like Gene and mRNA synthesis through platforms such as BIOXP from Telesis Bio. We offer a wide range of R&D services from Design to proof of level concept and even hand-held to scale up projects from various fields. We have been providing professional consulting services to clients all over India with respect to plant health testing. We would also embark on development of Diagnostic kits as relevant to the Agri and Human Diagnostic Industry.
